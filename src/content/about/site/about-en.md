I use this site to write about software engineering, AI tools, product thinking, and work worth compounding over time.

This blog is built with Astro and Retypeset, with three goals:

- Keep the site fast, clean, and SEO-friendly.
- Make long-form reading feel deliberate, especially for Chinese content.
- Leave enough flexibility to grow into a mix of essays, project notes, and a personal knowledge base.

If you care about craft, clarity, and durable systems, you will probably find something useful here.
